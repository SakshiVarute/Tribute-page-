Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

The Founder of the Maratha Empire

From: 19 February 1630 - 3 April 1680

About Shivaji Maharaj

Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj was not just a warrior king, but a visionary leader who laid the foundation of a strong and just kingdom. He believed in courage, intelligence, and respect for all religions.

Born in the hill fort of Shivneri, Shivaji Maharaj grew up learning the values of freedom, discipline, and self-rule. These values later became the soul of the Maratha Empire.

Why He Is Remembered

  • Founder of the Maratha Empire
  • Master of guerrilla warfare
  • Great administrator and strategist
  • Respected women and protected the common people

A Legacy That Lives On

Shivaji Maharaj’s life teaches us that true leadership comes from character, not power. Even today, he continues to inspire millions to stand for justice, bravery, and self-respect.

“Freedom is a blessing that everyone has the right to receive.”

— Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj
